Our Vision

Power Ministries International, Inc., an indigenous movement incorporated in the year 1987 in USA is gearing up with a positive commitment to fulfill its VISION viz.,

  • To reach the unreached through Conventions and Crusades around the world
  • To provide Intensive Training for the Christian leaders To equip the Leaders of the independent Full Gospel churches
  • By motivating them to step up to the level of first century church started by the Apostles trained by our Lord and savior Jesus Christ.

Statement of Faith

We believe the Bible to be the inspired, infallible, and authoritative word of God given to men: it is the final authority in every area of our lives. We believe that there is one God eternally existent in three Persons, the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit. We believe in the deity of our Lord Jesus Christ, in His virgin birth, in His sinless life, in His miracles, in His atoning death, in His shed blood, in His bodily resurrection, in His Ascension to the right hand of the Father and His personal return in power and glory very soon. We believe that receiving Jesus Christ as one's personal Lord and Savior is the one and the only way for the salvation of lost and sinful man. We believe in the indwelling of the Holy Spirit upon receiving Jesus Christ as our personal Lord and Savior and in the baptism of the Holy Spirit, which empowers the believer to be a living witness for Jesus Christ.

We believe in the resurrection of both the saved and the lost; they that are saved to the resurrection of life with the Lord; and they that are lost to the resurrection of damnation. We believe in sanctification of heart and life by the blood of Christ which separates us from the love of the world.
